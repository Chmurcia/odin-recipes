Mmmm... Pizza! In the past - my favourite one. Now it is on second place, but let us go back to main topic.

What we need

  • 1/4 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1.2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1.2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1,5 cups of pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded monterey jack cheese
  • 3 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • 2,5 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fast rise yeast
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Recipe

  1. In large bowl mix flour, salt, sugar and fast rise yeast
  2. MIx water and oil; add to the flour mixture
  3. Turn onto floured surface; knead for 2 minutes
  4. Place ina greased bowl; turning on grease top
  5. Cover and let rise for 20 minutes
  6. Punch down; place on 12in, greased pizza pan
  7. Pat into a circle
  8. Topping: Mix first 5 ingredients and spread over crust
  9. Put a few pepperoni slices on thetop of sauce
  10. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the mozzarella; 1/2 of the monterey jack, and 1/2 of the parmesan
  11. Put the rest of the pepperoni on
  12. Repeat the cheese layer
  13. Bake at 400* for 20 minutes or until light brown
